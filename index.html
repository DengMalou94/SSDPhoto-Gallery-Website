SOUTH SUDAN BASKETBALL GALLERY

The South Sudan men's national basketball team, formed in 2016, is making its Olympic debut at the 2024 Games. Led by former NBA player Luol Deng, the team is a symbol of the nation's resilience and rapid growth in basketball. They qualified for the Olympics by finishing as the top-ranking African squad at the 2023 FIBA Basketball World Cup. The team's journey is a remarkable one, considering South Sudan gained independence only in 2011.
